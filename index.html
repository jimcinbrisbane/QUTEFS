About

The Queensland University of Tecnology Economics Finance Society ( QUTEFS ) is a student run organisation whose goal is to Inform, Inspire and Incorporate its members into the real world. EFS offers you invaluable networking opportunities with both industry professionals and students. It is a great way to make new friends, meet new people and get your foot well within the door. EFS has one a number of awards for it’s involvement with QUT and overall success as a student run society, including 2009, 2011 and 2013 QUT Club of the Year! The Economics and Finance Society (EFS) informs, inspires and incorporates its members into the real world. Through professional workshops, extensive networking events and seminars conducted, EFS positions its members effectively for future employment. The Queensland University of Tecnology Economics Finance Society’s corporate partners are also given the opportunity to promote their organisation, shape students and recruit new talent.

Who are we?

With a proud and distinguished history stretching back to the early years of the new millennium the QUT Economics and Finance Society is truly the premier society on campus for those students passionate about the disciplines of Economics and Finance. Our mission statement of EFS outlines three main goals.

Inspire

Few people know just what economics and finance is; fewer still are aware of the amazing opportunities the field presents. EFS seeks to inspire a love for economics and finance within its members; helping to establish a lifelong passion.

Inform

You won’t learn everything you need for business in a lecture hall; somethings can only be taught outside the classroom. EFS helps equip its members with critical knowledge needed for business success that the university won’t teach you. Our professional development workshops and industry seminars will teach you the skills needed to succeed in the business world.

Incorporate

They say only 20% of jobs are ever advertised, EFS can help find the jobs that aren’t. By attending EFS networking events members gain the opportunity to liaise with professionals in the field of economics and finance, human-relations personnel and even corporate CEOs. The contacts you make at EFS may not get you a job; but they can certainly help get your foot in the door.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why should I join QUT Economics and Finance Society?

A: Your Economics and Finance Society membership gives you access to the QUT Economics and Finance Societies tools and resources, invaluable employment opportunities and early-bird discounts to all of our events. Your membership is not only a financial investment in EFS but a lifetime investment in your future.

Q: Why do you charge a membership fee?

A: EFS charges a membership fee so we can finance the cost of our events. You can be assured that you will get your values worth through our numerous events each year.

Q: Can I sign up with QUT Economics and Finance Society at any other place?

A: You can become a member of the Economics Finance Society in-person at O-Week, Market Day or at the front door at our events. Alternatively, you can signup through the website.

Q: How do I join the EFS Executive?

A: EFS will notify you by Facebook, email and via our website whenever there are openings for executive positions. However, you must first be a member of the society for a semester before becoming an EFS Executive.
